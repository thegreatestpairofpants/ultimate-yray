The hit new game staring Isaac St. Michel

"Never have I experienced so much fun converting monkeys as Isaac" -You if you read this out loud

Isaac St. Michel in Africa: Monkey Converting Time is an all brand new game based off of a true story. The game takes place in the wild forests of Africa surrounding Isaac's house. The plot folllows Isaac as he goes on a mission to convert as many monkeys as he can to Christianity. However, Isaac encounters some challenges when spreading the word of god. Choose your own path, as you guide Isaac to fufiling his mission. Will you reach an ending where Isaac is burnt into ashes? Will you help him suceed in his missionary work? You'll have to play, in order to find out.


What does the game feature?

  • The monkeys poof into white people when Isaac converts them
  • Everything in the game is based off events that have happened, or will happen
  • Everything Isaac does in the game, he is fully capable of in real life
  • The game is in color, rather than black and white
  • Very accurate depictions of Isaac
  • Parts of the game takes place at Isaac's house
